Summary

Building a User-Friendly AI Art Platform

aiRight brings AI-generated art to life, allowing users to create, trade, and monetize their digital works. My focus in designing the platform was to make the experience engaging and straightforward—ensuring that users, whether artists or collectors, can easily navigate and interact with the tools available.

Dashboard

A Smooth AI Image Generation Process

Creating AI art should feel creative, not complicated. I designed an interface where users can quickly input prompts, experiment with different AI models, and see real-time previews. The layout prioritizes clarity, making adjustments and refinements effortless without overwhelming the user.

Sale flow

A Marketplace Designed for Discovery and Trading

Beyond creation, aiRight serves as a marketplace where AI-generated images and prompts can be bought and sold. The marketplace layout was structured to highlight artwork while keeping key actions—like minting, listing, and purchasing—accessible with minimal friction. A balance between visual appeal and usability was key in making transactions feel smooth.

Transparency

Encouraging Community Engagement

Beyond transactions, the NFT details page includes interactive elements like comments, shares, and social links. These features create a sense of community, allowing collectors and creators to connect and engage with AI art beyond just the sale.
